Our Story

Born and raised surrounded by nature, we always have been growing our own herbs. Best friends since we were 17 years old, we have travelled the world, exploring exotic places and going on adventures you can only do with a friend you trust. We were introduced to different tea cultures and were deeply inspired by the tea culture in Japan as well as in India. We love the richness and diverse world of tea.

Throughout our world travels, in the most desolate of places, we always found our way back home with a cup of tea. That’s Miss Tea’s soul.

We founded Miss Tea in 2008, in New York City, with a vision to bring awareness of the precious tea culture, to the people who live in the busiest city in the world.

As a women-owned company committed to honor the power of natural healing, and share with our community this simple ceremony that always brings a quiet moment and a peace of mind.

Out of a profound belief in the power of teas and herbs, we have created a plant-based lifestyle experience. Miss Tea offers loose leaf organic teas from all over the world, handcrafted signature herbal infusions that carry medicinal purposes, along with a line of wellness products that are made of plants — coming from the earth, comforting the body, relaxing the mind.

Trust your senses and enjoy the experience.
